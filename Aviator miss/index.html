Plinko – the gambling game

Plinko is a classic game that has been popularized by its appearance on the TV show «The Price is Right». It's a simple game where a ball is dropped from the top of a board filled with pegs, and the ball bounces around until it lands in one of several slots at the bottom. Players can win prizes based on which slot the ball lands in, with higher payouts for more difficult-to-reach slots.

Plinko BGAMING

The popular gaming software developer and crypto gambling pioneer BGAMING presents its version of the Plinko game.

On the right side of the screen, there is a pyramid-shaped field with lines of pegs, the number of which can be configured from 8 to 16. At the bottom of the pyramid, there are slots with different coefficients. When the ball hits such a slot, depending on its face value, the player either:

  • Wins if the coefficient x > 1;
  • Loses if x < 1;
  • Remains at their own if x = 1.

Under the playing field is the game control panel. In the center, there is a large «Play» button, with which the game starts and the ball falls from the top of the pyramid to its bottom, landing in one or another slot.

To the left of the button, there are switches for risk levels:

  • Low – it is the safest level of the game. There is only one slot that cuts your bet in half, and it is right in the middle of the slot line. There are also two «1» here, leaving you with your own, and the maximum win here is x16.

    This level is perhaps the most win-win, although the most boring. Here you will not earn large sums, but you will gradually move towards winnings. This level is suitable for beginners or cautious players who are used to not asking for trouble.

  • Normal – at this level, there are already 3 losing slots and the maximum win is x110 bet.
  • High – here you will need your entire reserve of luck because at this level there are as many as five losing slots out of 11. However, the maximum win in the case of 16 lines of pegs is bet x1,000, which, if the maximum bet is $100, will bring you a $99,900 net profit.

To the right of the «Play» button, there is a switch for manual or auto betting control. Under the button, you will find the bet functional. You can choose the bet rate from $1 to $100.

What is Plinko?

Plinko is a digital version of the classic game «The Price is Right» where players can drop a virtual ball onto a virtual board filled with pegs, with the opportunity to win real money based on where the ball lands in the slots at the bottom.

How does Plinko work?

The Plinko online casino game generally involves a game board with a grid of pegs and a series of slots at the bottom. The player places a bet and then releases a ball from the top of the board, which bounces off the pegs and eventually lands in one of these slots. Depending on the slot in which the ball landed, the player's bet amount is multiplied by a coefficient, which ultimately determines whether the bet has been played or not. Thus, the player either wins money or loses, or stays on their own.

The outcome of each game is determined by an RNG to ensure fairness.
